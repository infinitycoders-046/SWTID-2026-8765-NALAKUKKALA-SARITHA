📊 Project Overview

Strategic Product Placement Analysis using Tableau helps businesses understand how product positioning, pricing, promotions, consumer demographics, and foot traffic influence sales performance.

The project utilizes interactive Tableau dashboards to identify sales trends, compare product categories, evaluate promotional effectiveness, and support data-driven merchandising decisions.
